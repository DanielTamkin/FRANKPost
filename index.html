Chris McIvor

President & Executive Producer

Chris is FRANK Digital’s Executive Producer and CEO who works with all types of organizations to help tell stories that create change. Having travelled extensively in several third world and war-torn countries helped form who Chris is as a person and content producer, editor and director. His almost thirty year career as an shooter/editor, lead him to directing, which is a passion of his. Chris contributes to the industry and community by giving his time as a board member of On Screen Manitoba, Film Training Manitoba, and is a member of the Canadian Media Producers Association.


Fun Fact: Chris loves to move and has moved homes 8 times in 4 years.


Lawrence Garry

Director, Post Production

Our longest serving FRANK Digital team member, Lawrence has more than 34 years experience in both film and broadcast television. He’s been involved in the creation and production of many local, national, and international television commercials and promotional videos. Some of our clients prefer to work only with Lawrence; they include The Canadian Curling Association, Oreck and Hummer Productions. Lawrence is also the Technical Director of Great Tastes of Manitoba, FRANK Digital’s long serving cooking television series.


Fun Fact: Lawrence wears cowboy boots every day.


Andrew Shire

Senior Colourist
Manager, Post Services

Andrew has more than 18 years of experience in the industry. During this time, Andrew has been at the forefront of post production technology, and has worked on hundreds of corporate, commercial and film projects. An expert in Avid Media Composer, Avid shared production workflows, Adobe Premiere, and DaVinci Resolve, he is FRANK's digital Yoda! Andrew’s understanding of underlying post production infrastructure has allowed him to design efficient digital post production workflows for feature film studios like FOX, Universal and Sony along with television productions for Sky Broadcasting, CBC, PBS, BBC, Discovery and the Smithsonian.


Fun Fact: Andrew is a drone and RC plane pilot.


Howard Rissin

Chief Audio Engineer

With more than 35 years experience in the music and post-production industries, Howie’s skills and knowledge have brought him accolades in both. Having been a part of the Vancouver and Winnipeg production communities, he has put his creative touch on hundreds of projects, including award winning albums, TV shows and films. Over the last 15 years he has mixed dozens of projects in 5.1 surround for Canadian and International broadcasters. Howie is a graduate of Music Industry Arts from Fanshawe College.


Fun Fact: Howie has 3 platinum records from the 80’s in Vancouver (when he had big hair).


Libby Lea

Vice President, Production

Libby is a motivated leader who is focused on solving challenges and passionate about producing the best projects possible. She is a people person who is a good listener and utilizes these skills to build real, effective relationships. A career highlight was working on a documentary about the Athabasca Dene people and spending time up North learning about the Caribou People and their traditions.


Fun Fact: Libby used to do singing telegrams as Marilyn Monroe.
